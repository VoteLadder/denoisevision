Study Instructions

About This Study

You'll be evaluating pairs of chest X-ray images. For each pair:

  • The left image is a reference (noisy) image
  • The right image is either the original or a denoised version

Your Task

For each image pair, you'll need to:

  1. Rate the overall quality improvement of the new image when c/w noisy image (1-5)
  2. Indicate whether you think the comparison is original or denoised
  3. Optionally provide additional comments

Study Design

This study uses optimal statistical power design with:

  • 80 unique images to evaluate
  • 15% duplicate images to measure consistency
  • Randomized presentation order

This configuration provides optimal statistical power to detect a 10% difference in image quality.
